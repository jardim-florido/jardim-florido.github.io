  • 'jardim florido'




    To play with the diverse flowers (generative processes) please press the following keys in your keyboard:

    Q W E R T Y A S D F and 1 2 3

    'jardim florido' is an ongoing exploration and space of encounter for concepts to be alive, where thought in motion activates potential in the force of form that shape experience, where concepts are lived and explored through abstract visual worlds of relation that pulsate in their own way. These generative processes have been created by writing algorithms and their transformational essence has a concern and ainity with the creation of transversal trajectories, rather than static objects; the intersectional place from where these processes emerge is from an active practice of writing 'algorithms as thoughts' (TOPLAP ManifestoDraft).

    This space has been created for readers and writers who at the moment of crossing this threshold their roles become blurred with the possibility of creatively cocompose, especially through keyboard real-time input. This aspect is an opportunity to explore 'new ways of coming into relation in the digital space through processes that moves us as we move them. An essential aspect of this project lies in the exploration and implementation of 'forms of coding that can deviate from what we see currently on digital platforms, which is intensely individualized self-presenting and relentless self-promoting' (Manning, 2017). This nook in the web is a place where multiplicity is celebrated, and from where potential can be transformed through movements of thought.

    Rodrigo Velasco yecto - 2020 (in progress).
